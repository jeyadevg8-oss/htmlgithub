The Samsung Galaxy S25 is a series of high-end Android-based smartphones developed and marketed by Samsung Electronics as part of its flagship Galaxy S Series.[8][9] They collectively serve as the successor to the Galaxy S24 series. The S25, S25+ and S25 Ultra models were announced on January 22, 2025, at the Galaxy Unpacked event in San Jose, California, and were released on February 7, 2025.[10]
